About Our Club

Preserving Bengali culture and traditions in Pune

Our Story

Founded in 2018, Agradoot Bangosamaj has been at the forefront of preserving and promoting Bengali culture in Pune. What started as a small gathering of Bengali families has grown into a vibrant community of over 1200 members.

Our mission is to celebrate the divine feminine power through traditional Durga Puja rituals while fostering a sense of community among Bengalis and culture enthusiasts in Pune. We believe in the power of cultural exchange and the importance of passing down traditions to future generations.

Our Values

  • Devotion: Deep respect for spiritual traditions and rituals
  • Community: Building strong bonds within our cultural family
  • Preservation: Keeping Bengali culture alive for future generations
  • Inclusivity: Welcoming everyone to experience our rich heritage
